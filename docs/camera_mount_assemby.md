# On-wrist Camera Mount Assembly

1. With a soldering iron, place 2x M3 inserts here:
![Step 1](/assets/images/camera-mount-step1.png) 

2. Then place 2x M3x25 screws here, used to adjust the angle of the camera, plus 2 nuts to lock the screw positions:
![Step 2](/assets/images/camera-mount-step2.png)

3. Put the mobile support in the base and secure the axis with two self-tapping 3x8mm screws (thermoplastic screws):
![Step 3](/assets/images/camera-mount-step3.png)

4. Then place the camera's back holes in front of the holes of the mobile support part and put in 2x M3x6 screws:
![Step 4](/assets/images/camera-mount-step4.png)


5. Put in 4x self-tapping 2.5x6mm screws:
![Step 5](/assets/images/camera-mount-step5.png)
